## Post 1
**Date:** 4 days ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Cold call opener framework

250+ sales teams. 10,000+ reps. The best cold call opener has 3 parts:

1. Greeting — answer who you are
2. The Three Ts — Trigger (timely signal), Tension (relevant problem), Trust (social proof)
3. Permission — permission-based questions outperform any other opener by 5x (Gong, 300k+ calls)

Example: "Hi Kate, noticed you're hiring MIG welders, not sure if you're running into challenges automating low volume welds like Caterpillar has. Got a min?"

## Post 2
**Date:** 6 days ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Inbound vs outbound team separation

Stop trying to convert your inbound team to outbound. Key insight from Tara DiCristo-Schmitt (CRO at Houzz, former VP Sales HubSpot):

- Keep inbound team intact
- For outbound, hire net new — build separately with people who want to do it
- You can teach skill. You cannot teach will.
- Reps hired for inbound were promised no cold calling — retraining rarely works
