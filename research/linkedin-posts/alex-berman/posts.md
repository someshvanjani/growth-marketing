## Post 1
**Date:** 7 hours ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** What makes cold email campaigns succeed or fail

Across 500,000+ meetings and 14,000 clients, the difference between 2% and 25% meeting book rates:

1. Sell a new and needed offer — $700k from 41 emails by matching offer to a documentary format podcast guests already paid for
2. Breaking news — $100k from 4 emails by framing Magento 1 end-of-support as urgent threat
3. Get famous first — $600k ARR in 30 days by becoming #1 on an industry directory before outreaching

Subject lines barely matter. Targeting and offer construction are everything.

## Post 2
**Date:** 1 day ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Two things that actually move cold email results

1. Fix targeting — filter by department size not company size, verify employment status, verify emails
2. Fix deliverability — use custom SMTP not Gmail/Outlook, distribute across multiple domains, max 2 emails/day per inbox

Not personalization tricks. Not subject line tweaks. Infrastructure and list quality first.

## Post 3
**Date:** 2 days ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Write manually before using Clay

Clay is a scaling tool, not a research tool. Before setting up any AI personalization:
- Write 30-50 cold emails manually
- Look at real prospects on Google Maps, LinkedIn, company sites
- Find data points that resonate — then scale those with Clay

One example: complimenting the view from a prospect's office window (found via Google Maps) — CEOs spend weeks choosing their office. It converts.
