## Post 1
**Date:** 5 hours ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Why booked meetings are the wrong metric

Conversation with a founder: agency ran outbound for 6 months, booked 22 meetings, only 2 turned into real pipeline.

Key insight: booked meetings are the biggest scam in outbound sales. Agencies optimize for meetings to show progress — it tells you nothing about real market response.

Completed conversations are the only metric that matters. They give you Yes / No / Not Me / Not Now with real context.

## Post 2
**Date:** 2 days ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Phone number scoring case study

Same list of 2,245 phone numbers. Old approach: 1 conversation per 60 dials (1.6% connect rate).

After scoring numbers for phone intent (likelihood to pick up):
- 21 conversations from 53 dials = 39.6% connect rate
- An entire month of conversations in 2 hours of dialing
- Same list, not a single new name added

Insight: The list decides the outcome before a rep even picks up the phone. Blaming reps is wrong — score for reachability first.
