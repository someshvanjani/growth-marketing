## Post 1
**Date:** 3 days ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Pre and post call flows for cold traffic

You don't have a real outbound operation if you can't convert cold to paying.
Most teams book the meeting but fail on show-up rate and close rate.

Pro tips after booking 1,000s of meetings:
1. Set up pre-call email drip post-booking with heavy social proof
2. Set day-of email/text reminders
3. Post-call, enroll prospect in nurture flow

## Post 2
**Date:** 5 days ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Follow-up persistence

50%+ of outbound success is following up until you make the sale or get told to stop.

Example: Person cold called 3 times over weeks — 27 min conversation on attempt 3. Most sellers stop after attempt 1.

Rule: Re-engage leads 30 days post-initial touch if no explicit "not interested." Timing readiness changes.

## Post 3
**Date:** 6 days ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Cold email playbook shift in 2026

Cold email in 2026 has moved to:
1. Soft CTA in email (can I send more info? can I show you how I'd do this for [company]?)
2. Enrich for phone number → call within 30 mins of positive reply
3. For qualified leads, book directly on the call

Reason: inboxes are crowded, soft CTA + phone follow-up is proving to be the highest converting approach.
