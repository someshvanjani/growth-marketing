## Post 1
**Date:** 1 week ago (June 2026)
**Topic:** Inbox management and mental clarity for founders

Had 675+ emails sitting in Gmail — not from lack of care but from constant
context switching across client, newsletter, podcast, family, and partnership
threads. Started testing Fyxer AI email organizer. Key insight: the real
value isn't the AI, it's the mental clarity. Founders underestimate how much
energy gets burned from tiny bits of friction — an inbox is hundreds of small
decisions per day, and those decisions compound.
